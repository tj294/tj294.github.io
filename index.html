Welcome to Haunted Exeter

The home of Spooks and Scares

Haunted Exeter is the South-West's Premier ghost-hunting organisation. Started by Tom "Spaghetti Arms" Joshi-Cale and Christina "Rat Hands" Williams, it is Haunted Exeter's mission to find and eliminate all ghosts, poltergeists and other apparations from the earthly plane - but no further north than Tiverton (we can travel to Taunton if there's a cricket match on).
