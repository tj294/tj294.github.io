Introduction

Haunted Exeter are proud to present our flagship product in ghost detection, the Electro-Magnetic Field (EMF) detector. With three Physics degrees between our team, we felt uniquely placed to design the latest and most effective EMF detector available on the market.

For those of you who are unaware, EMF detectors take local readings of electromagnetic fields and display them in an easy-to-interpret manner. Since ghosts affect EMF fields, then an EMF detector can help any aspiring ghost hunter to pinpoint the location of a spiritual presence, whether visible or not.
